# Sound Assets for Immersive Reader

This folder contains sound files for the immersive 3D book reading experience.

## Available Sound Files

### Page Turn Effect
- `page-turn.mp3` - Short paper rustling sound (~20KB)

### Ambient Sounds
- `ambiance-fireplace.mp3` - Crackling fireplace loop (~3.7MB)
- `ambiance-rain.mp3` - Gentle rain loop (~2.8MB)
- `ambiance-library.mp3` - Quiet library ambiance loop (~9.5MB)

## Usage

These sounds are automatically used by the Immersive Reader:

1. **Page Turn Sound**: Plays when flipping pages (if sound effects are enabled)
2. **Ambient Sounds**: Can be enabled from the sound settings panel in the reader

## Sound Controls

In the Immersive Reader:
- Click the volume icon in the bottom control bar to toggle sound effects
- Open the sound settings panel to choose ambient sounds and adjust volume
